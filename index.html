About Me

I am a 20 year old sports lover who was born in Balston Spa, New York. Although I was born in New York I only lived there for about four years and subsequently moved to Cranston, Rhode Island. We've just moved to Narragansett in this previous February and are right on the coast next to the beach.

He purchased the store my sophomore year of high school and has turned it into a highly succesful business. Hopefully next semester I will be able to get a job on campus through work study to make some side money.
